# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Montreal diners choosing where to spend a serious evening, mostly arriving on a phone after seeing the restaurant on Instagram. They already expect good steak; what they're deciding is whether this room is worth the occasion. The site's job is seduction, not information — the reservation is the result of the mood, not the headline.

## Product Purpose
A single cinematic homepage for Sel Noir, a dark-luxe steakhouse at 1490 Sherbrooke, Montreal. The name means "black salt"; the whole experience is built on a black canvas lit by ember, salt, and live fire. The site should feel like the moment the door opens — hushed, expensive, alive — and unfold like one uninterrupted camera move. It replaces a generic WordPress template that undersold a luxury room.

## Brand Personality
Midnight, intimate, expensive, alive. Noir film title card meets a fine-dining menu letterpressed on heavy black stock. Confident and quiet — it never shouts. Bilingual-ready (FR/EN, Montreal).

## Anti-references
- Generic restaurant WordPress templates (the thing it replaces): stock hero, doubled sections, PDF menu.
- SaaS-cream editorial-magazine slop: warm off-white bg, Cormorant/Playfair italics, ruled three-column metadata, eyebrow on every section.
- Bright, busy, "appetizing" food-delivery aesthetics. This is dark, not hungry-bright.
- Gold-foil-gradient luxury cliché. Restraint reads more expensive than shine.

## Design Principles
- **The website is the first course.** Atmosphere over information; the mood does the selling.
- **One uninterrupted camera move.** Sections are scenes in a single sequence, not a list of blocks. No hard cuts.
- **Let the fire and salt be the design.** Full-bleed motion imagery carries the page; chrome recedes.
- **Voltage, used once.** Near-black everywhere, ember/gold as the single accent. Discipline = luxury.
- **Felt but fast.** Every effect degrades to a calm, still-beautiful static version under reduced motion or weak hardware.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` (pause scrubbed/looping video, show stills, drop scroll choreography). Maintain ≥4.5:1 for body copy over dark backgrounds; ember-gold accents used at large sizes only. Keyboard-visible focus states. Descriptive alt/aria text in the brand voice. Bilingual FR/EN intended.
